About Sponsorship

Help support our mission to create a vegan future at the heart of UTD.

This year we are hoping to grow the reach of Veg Fest by doubling the length of our event and adding in attention grabbing activities such as a film booth and exciting raffles. We hope you can join us in creating a vegan future!

Not only will you get your beautiful logo on our sponsors page, you will also get direct exposure to everyone who attends The UTD Fest through our promotional email. By donating samples and/or food, UTD students will become aware of your business and will so how great it is; that means sales for you.

The purpose of this Veg Fest is to promote a world without animal farming. In sponsoring it, you will be furthering that promotion and making that new world more attractive for those who have yet to come around.
